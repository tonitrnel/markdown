// Underscore emphasis with CJK punctuation
// Under CJK-friendly rules, CJK punctuation is NOT treated as non-CJK punctuation,
// so _ can open/close emphasis adjacent to CJK punctuation sequences.

## CJK Underscore: CJK punctuation adjacency

```````````````````````````````` example
__注意__：注意事項
.
<p><strong>注意</strong>：注意事項</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
注意：__注意事項__
.
<p>注意：<strong>注意事項</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````

## CJK Underscore: CJK ambiguous punctuation sequences (SVS fullwidth form)

```````````````````````````````` example
正體字。︁_Traditional._
.
<p>正體字。︁<em>Traditional.</em></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
正體字。︁__Hong Kong and Taiwan.__
.
<p>正體字。︁<strong>Hong Kong and Taiwan.</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
简体字 / 新字体。︀_Simplified._
.
<p>简体字 / 新字体。︀<em>Simplified.</em></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
简体字 / 新字体。︀__Mainland China or Japan.__
.
<p>简体字 / 新字体。︀<strong>Mainland China or Japan.</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
"︁Git"︁__Hub__
.
<p>&quot;︁Git&quot;︁<strong>Hub</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````
