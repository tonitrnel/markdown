## Images (extension)

OFM supports image dimensions in standard Markdown image alt text.

Image with width and height:

```````````````````````````````` example
![Engelbart|120x160](https://history-computer.com/ModernComputer/Basis/images/Engelbart.jpg)
.
<p><img src="https://history-computer.com/ModernComputer/Basis/images/Engelbart.jpg" alt="Engelbart" width="120" height="160" /></p>
````````````````````````````````

Image with width:

```````````````````````````````` example
![grapefruit|100](https://interactive-examples.mdn.mozilla.net/media/cc0-images/grapefruit-slice-332-332.jpg)
.
<p><img src="https://interactive-examples.mdn.mozilla.net/media/cc0-images/grapefruit-slice-332-332.jpg" alt="grapefruit" width="100" /></p>
````````````````````````````````
