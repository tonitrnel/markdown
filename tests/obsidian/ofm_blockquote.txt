## Block Quotes (extension)

OFM block quotes support lazy continuation lines without `>` until a blank line.
An explicit `>` blank line keeps following lazy continuation inside the same quote.

Block quote lazy continuation after explicit blank quote line:

```````````````````````````````` example
> bar
>
baz
.
<blockquote>
<p>bar</p>
<p>baz</p>
</blockquote>
````````````````````````````````

Blank line without `>` ends a block quote:

```````````````````````````````` example
> bar

baz
.
<blockquote>
<p>bar</p>
</blockquote>
<p>baz</p>
````````````````````````````````
