// GFM strikethrough and table tests under CJK-friendly mode
// These are non-CJK content and should behave identically to standard GFM.

## CJK GFM: Strikethrough (non-CJK content)

```````````````````````````````` example
~~Hi~~ Hello, ~there~ world!
.
<p><del>Hi</del> Hello, <del>there</del> world!</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
This ~~has a

new paragraph~~.
.
<p>This ~~has a</p>
<p>new paragraph~~.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
This will ~~~not~~~ strike.
.
<p>This will ~~~not~~~ strike.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
A proper ~strikethrough~.
.
<p>A proper <del>strikethrough</del>.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
These are ~not strikethroughs.
.
<p>These are ~not strikethroughs.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
No, they are not~
.
<p>No, they are not~</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
This ~is ~ legit~ isn't ~ legit.
.
<p>This <del>is ~ legit</del> isn't ~ legit.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
This is not ~~~~~one~~~~~ huge strikethrough.
.
<p>This is not ~~~~~one~~~~~ huge strikethrough.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
~one~ ~~two~~ ~~~three~~~
.
<p><del>one</del> <del>two</del> ~~~three~~~</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
No ~mismatch~~
.
<p>No <del>mismatch</del>~</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
~~**_`this`_**~~  
~~***`this`***~~  
~~___`this`___~~
.
<p><del><strong><em><code>this</code></em></strong></del><br />
<del><em><strong><code>this</code></strong></em></del><br />
<del><em><strong><code>this</code></strong></em></del></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
~~**_this_**~~  
~~***this***~~  
~~___this___~~
.
<p><del><strong><em>this</em></strong></del><br />
<del><em><strong>this</strong></em></del><br />
<del><em><strong>this</strong></em></del></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
~Hi~ Hello, world!
.
<p><del>Hi</del> Hello, world!</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
This ~text~ ~~is~~ ~~~curious~~~.
.
<p>This <del>text</del> <del>is</del> ~~~curious~~~.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
[^~~is~~1]
.
<p>[^~~is~~1]</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
a~~()a~~a
.
<p>a~~()a~~a</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
a~~a()~~a
.
<p>a~~a()~~a</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
~~()~~
.
<p><del>()</del></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
(~~()~~)
.
<p>(<del>()</del>)</p>
````````````````````````````````

## CJK GFM: Table (non-CJK content)

```````````````````````````````` example
| A |
| - |
| https://example.com/ |
.
<table>
<thead>
<tr>
<th>A</th>
</tr>
</thead>
<tbody>
<tr>
<td>https://example.com/</td>
</tr>
</tbody>
</table>
````````````````````````````````
