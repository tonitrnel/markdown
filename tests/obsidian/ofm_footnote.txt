## Footnotes (extension)

OFM supports referenced footnotes, lazy continuation lines, and inline footnotes.

Referenced and inline footnotes:

```````````````````````````````` example
Footnotes[^1] and named[^note]. Inline ^[inline *note*].

[^1]: First footnote.
[^note]: Named footnote.
Continued line.
.
<p>Footnotes<a href="#cont-fn-1" id="cont-fn-ref-1">[1]</a> and named<a href="#cont-fn-note" id="cont-fn-ref-note">[2]</a>. Inline <a href="#cont-fn-inline-footnote-3" id="cont-fn-ref-inline-footnote-3">[3]</a>.</p>
<section>
<h2>Footnotes</h2>
<ol>
<li id="cont-fn-1">
<p>First footnote.<a href="#cont-fn-ref-1">↩</a></p>
</li>
<li id="cont-fn-note">
<p>Named footnote.
Continued line.<a href="#cont-fn-ref-note">↩</a></p>
</li>
<li id="cont-fn-inline-footnote-3">
<p>inline <em>note</em><a href="#cont-fn-ref-inline-footnote-3">↩</a></p>
</li>
</ol>
</section>
````````````````````````````````
