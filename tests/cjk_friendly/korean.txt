// CJK-friendly emphasis with Korean (Hangul) characters
// Hangul script is classified as CJK character per the specification

## CJK Korean: Strong emphasis with Hangul

```````````````````````````````` example
**이 [링크](https://example.kr/)**만을 강조하고 싶다.
.
<p><strong>이 <a href="https://example.kr/">링크</a></strong>만을 강조하고 싶다.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
**스크립트(script)**라고
.
<p><strong>스크립트(script)</strong>라고</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
패키지를 발행하려면 **`npm publish`**를 실행하십시오.
.
<p>패키지를 발행하려면 <strong><code>npm publish</code></strong>를 실행하십시오.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
**안녕(hello)**하세요.
.
<p><strong>안녕(hello)</strong>하세요.</p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
ᅡ**(a)**
.
<p>ᅡ<strong>(a)</strong></p>
````````````````````````````````

```````````````````````````````` example
**(k)**ᄏ
.
<p><strong>(k)</strong>ᄏ</p>
````````````````````````````````
